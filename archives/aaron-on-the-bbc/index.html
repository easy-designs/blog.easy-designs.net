Aaron on the BBC (and UIE)

Posted by Kelly McCarthy on 03 August 2012 | Permalink

Aaron’s been all over the place lately. He was recently interviewed for a segment on HTML5 that ran on BBC television in the UK. True to form, his hour-long conversation boiled down to a few sound bites, but it was still an amazing opportunity.

In advance of his workshop at User Interface 17 this coming November, Aaron also sat down for a chat with Jared Spool to talk about responsive design, adaptive user interfaces, mobile and more. You can check out the podcast on the User Interface Engineering blog.
